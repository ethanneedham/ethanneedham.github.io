Ethan Needham is an American singer, songwriter, musician, and record producer. Following his departure from indie rock band The Pointbreaks in 2025, he has been developing a catalog of material centered around live performance.


His shows range from stripped-down acoustic sets to full band arrangements, shaped around venue, audience, and atmosphere. Each performance is designed for impact, flexibility, and emotional presence.
